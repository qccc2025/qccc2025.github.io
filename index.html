Quantum and Classical Computation and Complexity

The 3rd International Conference on Quantum and Classical Computation and Complexity (QCCC2025) is devoted to the development of quantum and classical algorithms in different areas of theoretical computer science in various models of computation, analysis of the complexity of algorithms and their comparison for quantum and classical versions

Potential Topics

  • quantum algorithms
  • models of quantum computation
  • quantum complexity theory
  • simulation of quantum systems
  • quantum cryptography
  • quantum communication
  • quantum information theory
  • quantum and classical machine learning
  • quantum and classical big data analysis
  • quantum and classical automata theory
